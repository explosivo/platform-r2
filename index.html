Platform-r2

SciFi RPG. So far its just a combat prototype.

Download Nightly Build (if you want a physical representation of the current code)

Download Combat Prototype (if you want to fight zombies like back in Pre-Alpha 0.2.6)

Controls

WASD to move

mouse to aim and fire laser gun

M to mute and unmute music

Tips

using weapons drains stamina

killing zombies gives you XP to gain levels

gaining Levels increases your stamina, health, and speed

for fps stats run in terminal Java -jar platform-r2.jar
